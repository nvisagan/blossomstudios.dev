## Why FaviGrab?

If you've ever needed to grab a website's favicon for a design mockup, a bookmark manager, or a development project, you know the pain. Digging through page source, guessing favicon paths, dealing with different formats and sizes.

FaviGrab makes it effortless. One click, and you have the favicon downloaded in high quality. It's the kind of tool that does one thing and does it well.

## How It Works

FaviGrab reads the current tab's favicon data using Chrome's built-in favicon API. When you click the extension icon, it presents available sizes and lets you download with a single click. Files are automatically named after the source website for easy organization.

## Built for Developers & Designers

Whether you're building a link aggregator, designing a browser mockup, or just collecting icons for reference, FaviGrab fits seamlessly into your workflow. No accounts, no subscriptions, no nonsense.
