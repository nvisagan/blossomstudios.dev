## Built for the Floor of a Card Show

TCGIQ replaces guesswork with the only number that matters: what cards have actually been selling for, right now. Pull a card up, pick its condition, and see the real low, median, and high from recent sales — plus the five most recent sold listings backing the range.

## Who It's For

Dealers working a booth, collectors hunting deals, traders mid-negotiation. Anyone who needs a fast, honest answer to "what's this actually worth?" without scrolling through stale comps or generic market averages.

## Why Fair Range

Most pricing tools quote a single market average — an opaque number that smooths over condition, recency, and outliers. Fair Range surfaces the spread you actually need to negotiate: low, median, and high from real recent sales, by condition, with the listings shown so you can verify the math yourself.
