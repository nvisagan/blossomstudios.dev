## Lock In and Get It Done

Lock In Timer lives in Chrome's side panel — always accessible, never in the way. Whether you use the Pomodoro Technique, time-blocking, or your own focus method, it adapts to how you work.

## Who It's For

Students cramming for exams. Developers deep in a codebase. Writers hitting a deadline. Anyone who wants a distraction-free timer that just works, without signing up for yet another productivity app.

## What's New in v2.0

Version 2.0 is a major upgrade: pause and resume support, a reset button, custom timer lengths, persistent state that survives closing the panel, full session statistics with daily streaks, and Chrome notifications so you never miss a timer — even from another tab.
