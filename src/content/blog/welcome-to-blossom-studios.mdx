We're Blossom Studios, a small indie studio building tools that developers and designers actually want to use.

## Our Philosophy

We believe the best tools are the ones that disappear into your workflow. No bloat, no unnecessary features, no dark patterns. Just focused software that does its job well and gets out of your way.

Every product we ship follows three principles:

1. **Do one thing well.** We'd rather build a tool that's exceptional at a single task than mediocre at many.
2. **Respect your resources.** Minimal permissions, tiny footprints, no tracking. Your computer and your data are yours.
3. **Ship and iterate.** We launch early, listen to feedback, and improve continuously.

## What We're Building

Our first release is [FaviGrab](/products/favigrab/), a Chrome extension that lets you download any website's favicon in one click. It's small, fast, and free.

We have more tools in the pipeline. Stay tuned by checking back on this blog or following us on [GitHub](https://github.com/blossomstudios).

## Open to Feedback

We're building in public and we want to hear from you. If you have ideas, feature requests, or just want to say hello, reach out on GitHub or X.
